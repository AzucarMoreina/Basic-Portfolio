About Me

Having grown up in the city and attending school out of state taught me the best of both worlds. I was street smart but also book smart.

Currently I am in school wanting to create a career for myself in the technical field. Although I am a newbie I am driven by one of the most powerful sources, motherhood. Ultimately I wish to create a life where I am both present in my childs life and happy with my work/life balance.
